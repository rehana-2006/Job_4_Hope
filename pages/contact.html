Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our platform and services

Click on the "Register" button in the top right corner, select "Parent" as your user type, and fill out the registration form with your personal information and skills. Once registered, you can create a detailed profile showcasing your abilities and start browsing job opportunities.
Register as an employer or NGO on our platform. After verification, you'll gain access to post job listings, specify required skills, and connect directly with qualified parents seeking employment.
No, our platform is completely free for parents seeking employment. For employers and NGOs, we offer both free basic listings and premium features to enhance visibility and reach.
We have a rigorous verification process that includes document verification, background checks, and ongoing monitoring to ensure all employers meet our safety and fairness standards.
The most common skills in demand include cooking, tailoring, cleaning, childcare, teaching, handicrafts, and basic construction work. We regularly update our platform with new opportunities across various skill categories.
You can report suspected child labour through our "Report Incident" page or by calling the National Child Helpline at 1098, which is available 24/7. All reports are treated confidentially and investigated promptly.
Yes, we partner with various NGOs and training organizations to offer skill development programs for parents. These programs help enhance existing skills and learn new ones to improve employment prospects.
Once logged in, you can access your dashboard where all your job applications are tracked. You'll receive notifications about application status updates and can view the complete history of your interactions with employers.
